What is DPG.GOV.BD?

DPG.gov.bd hosts a set of tools and softwares that intend to allow the government of Bangladesh, enterprises, entrepreneurs, and software developers, alongside their international counterparts, to use these digital Infrastructures to advance towards a national and international sustainable development. This is accomplished by resolving many issues plaguing nations worldwide relating to the deliverance and ubiquitous presence of government services, open data, and useful software. The open nature of these products allows all people to come forth and integrate them into developing better and more useful services.

Benefits of Joining DPG.GOV.BD

Joining our platform has many benefits, both on an individual/business level, as well as on a national and global context

  • Assisting government services and products to become more easily accessible, benefiting the nation overall to accelerate growth
  • Promotion of your product through DPG.gov.bd website, blog, mailing lists and relevant media outlets, leading to national and international prominence.
  • Mentorship, consultation and funding from DPG.gov.bd
  • Additional funding from being discovered by dpg.gov.bd partners and funders.
  • Collaboration opportunities to promote company, products, and services

DPG Standard

The Digital Public Goods Standard is a set of specifications and guidelines designed to maximise consensus about whether a digital solution conforms to the definition of a digital public good(SDGs). This definition stems from the UN Secretary-General’s Roadmap for Digital Cooperation.
